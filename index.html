Before You Go — Essentials
History · Apps · Food · Negotiation · Packing
⚔️
Who Were the Rajputs?
  • Warrior-aristocrat clans — Mewar, Marwar, Kachwaha (Jaipur)
  • Resisted Mughals for decades, then negotiated alliances
  • Amber Fort = Rajput kings commanding Mughal armies while building Hindu temples
🏰
The Fort System
  • Amber = royal palace · Jaigarh = armory + guns · Nahargarh = watchtower
  • All three connected — functioned as one defense system
  • Secret tunnel: Amber → Jaigarh (royal escape route)
📱
Essential Apps
  • IRCTC Rail Connect — train booking
  • Rapido / Ola — autos in Jaipur
  • Google Maps — download Jaipur offline before leaving Delhi
🍽️
Must Eat
  • Dal Baati Churma — soul of Rajasthani food

Mountain Essentials — Before You Go
Altitude · Packing · Food · Apps · What to Know
⛰️
Altitude + Weather
  • Mussoorie is 2,005m, Landour 2,270m, Lal Tibba 2,275m
  • Temperature: day 14–20°C, night 8–12°C (March/April)
  • Weather changes fast — mist can roll in within 20 minutes
  • First day: move slowly, drink water, let body adjust
🎒
Pack Smart
  • Thermal inner + 2 fleece layers + windproof outer jacket
  • Comfortable walking shoes — will walk 8–12km/day on uneven roads
  • Warm hat + gloves + scarf (mornings are cold)
  • Powerbank · small backpack · sunscreen (sun is stronger at altitude)
🍜
Must Eat in Mountains
  • Maggi at any hillside stall — non-negotiable mountain ritual
  • Garhwali thali: kafuli + aloo ke gutke + mandua roti
  • Rhododendron juice (buransh) — only in hills, only in spring
  • Anil's Café chai + toast at Char Dukan — life-changing
📱
Apps + Logistics
  • IRCTC Rail Connect — train booking
  • redBus — Volvo bus to Mussoorie
  • Google Maps — download Mussoorie offline
  • MakeMyTrip / Zostel — hotel booking for groups
🌲
Landour Secret
  • Most tourists never go past Mall Road — Landour is quieter, older, more beautiful
  • Ruskin Bond walks in Landour — keep eyes open near Char Dukan area
  • Char Dukan has been there for over 100 years — same four shops, same chai
🤝

  • Phone battery rule: everyone charges at night, no exceptions
